KW-tool Market

The platform btw KW-tool producers & consumers.

Pick the right apps and other KW-tools for your work. Our core tools will be free, and one subscription gives access to paid tools.

As a tool developer, you will profit based on the real usage amount of the tool.

Your KW Toolbox

Collection of your KW tools with your knowledge base.

The idea is to keep & use only the tools you need to get the work done. Tools are not important; Your life & work results are, period.

The platform for sharing and consuming knowledge.

Modern age global library: knowledge-focused, not entertainment. A monthly subscription gives access to all paid material.

Share knowledge as free to everyone or profit with it based on its consumption.
